Digitalni multimedij 1

Mihaela Kostanjski

Stranica je kreirana za 10. i 11. vježbu iz kolegija Digitalni multimedij 1

I went to the woods because I wanted to live deliberately. I wanted to live deep and suck out all the marrow of life. To put to rout all that was not life; and not, when I had come to die, discover that I had not lived. Ovo je citat iz filma Dead poets society iz 1989. godine.

Drugi citat iz istog filma u obliku blok citat:

I stand upon my desk
to remind myself that we must constantly look at things
in a different way.
